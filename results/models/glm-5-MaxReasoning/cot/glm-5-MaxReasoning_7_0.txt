## CSG Union Algorithm Analysis

For a CSG Union operation, we need to:
1. Identify the parts of mesh A that are outside mesh B
2. Identify the parts of mesh B that are outside mesh A
3. Find the intersection curves between the two meshes
4. Properly stitch everything together

### Key Components:

1. **Intersection Detection**: Find all triangle-triangle intersections between mesh A and mesh B
2. **Spatial Partitioning**: Use BVH or octree for efficient intersection queries
3. **Mesh Cutting**: Cut triangles along intersection curves
4. **Classification**: Determine which parts to keep (outside parts)
5. **Merging**: Combine the results into a single manifold mesh
