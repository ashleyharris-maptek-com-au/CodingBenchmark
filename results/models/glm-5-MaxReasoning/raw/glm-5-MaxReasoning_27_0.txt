**My Approach:**
1. **Small graphs (≤50 nodes)**: Backtracking with pruning for exact solutions
2. **Medium graphs (≤200k nodes)**: DSATUR algorithm (prioritizes vertices by saturation degree)
3. **Large graphs**: Simple greedy with Welsh-Powell ordering (by degree, largest first)

**Key optimizations:**
- Use bitmasks for k ≤ 64 for O(1) color availability checks
- Lazy deletion in DSATUR's priority queue to avoid complex updates
- Skip self-loops and invalid edges during input
