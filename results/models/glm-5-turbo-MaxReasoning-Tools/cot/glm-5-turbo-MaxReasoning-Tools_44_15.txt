The user wants a Gooch warm/cool shading fragment shader. Let me recall the Gooch shading model:

- Cool color = (0.2, 0.2, 0.75)
- Warm color = (0.7, 0.7, 0.4)
- t = dot(N, L) * 0.5 + 0.5
- Final color = mix(cool, warm, t)
- Alpha = 1.0

Where N is the surface normal and L is the light direction (normalized direction to the light).

The light position is given in ubo.lightPos.xyz, and the surface position is worldPos. So L = normalize(lightPos - worldPos).
