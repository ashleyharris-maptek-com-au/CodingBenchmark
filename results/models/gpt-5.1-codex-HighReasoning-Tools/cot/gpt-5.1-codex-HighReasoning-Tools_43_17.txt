**Generating HLSL shader for brick pattern**

I'm putting together HLSL code for a brick pattern with an 8x16 scale on UV coordinates, handling row offsets for mortar and computing mortar thickness on brick edges. I'll include Lambert shading using surface normals and a light direction, blend brick and mortar colors with smooth transitions, and optionally factor in per-vertex color for subtle variation. The approach avoids costly branching by relying on saturate and step functions where possible. I'm aiming for a clean, efficient pixel shader that fits the user's specification perfectly.
