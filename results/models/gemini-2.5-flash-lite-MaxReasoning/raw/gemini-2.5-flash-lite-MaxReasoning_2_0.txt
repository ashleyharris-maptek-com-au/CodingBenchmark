I have implemented the Chinese Postman Problem solver in C#. The solution is contained in `Program.cs` and includes:

1.  **Graph Representation**: Efficient adjacency list using lists of tuples.
2.  **Odd Degree Detection**: Identifying all vertices with an odd degree.
3.  **Shortest Path Calculation**: Dijkstra's algorithm with a priority queue for efficient distance and predecessor calculation.
4.  **Matching**: A greedy minimum weight matching approach to pair odd-degree nodes.
5.  **Phantom Edges**: Adding the shortest paths between matched nodes as phantom edges to ensure the graph is Eulerian.
6.  **Eulerian Circuit**: Hierholzer's algorithm to traverse the resulting Eulerian graph, handling multi-edges correctly.

The solution is modular and designed to be scalable.
