Let me analyze the Phong lighting formula:
- N: normal (world-space, interpolated)
- L: light direction (from fragment to light)
- V: view direction (from fragment to camera)
- R: reflection vector = reflect(-L, N)
- diffuse = max(dot(N, L), 0)
- specular = pow(max(dot(R, V), 0), 32)
- Output: 0.2 + 0.5*diffuse + 0.8*specular with alpha 1.0

3. Calculate view direction: from fragment to camera (cameraPos - worldPos)
4. Normalize both directions
5. Calculate reflection vector
