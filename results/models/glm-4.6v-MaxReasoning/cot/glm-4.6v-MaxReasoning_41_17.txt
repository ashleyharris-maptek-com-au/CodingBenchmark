Let me think about the brick pattern:
- Scale: 8x16 UV scale
- Staggered rows (half-brick offset every other row)
- Mortar thickness: 0.05 in X and 0.1 in Y
- Mortar color: (0.7, 0.7, 0.7)
- Brick color: (0.7, 0.2, 0.1)
- Lambertian shading with factor (0.4 + 0.6*diffuse)

For the brick pattern:
1. I'll scale the UV coordinates by 8 in X and 16 in Y
2. I'll calculate the brick position and whether it's a mortar joint
3. I'll handle the staggered rows by offsetting every other row by 0.5
4. I'll apply the mortar thickness

For Lambertian shading:
1. I need to calculate the light direction
2. I need to calculate the normal (which should be normalized)
3. I need to calculate the diffuse component
4. I'll apply the shading factor (0.4 + 0.6*diffuse)
