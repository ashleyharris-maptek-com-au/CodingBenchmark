The GLSL compute shader for gravitational N-body simulation has been written to 'nbody.comp'.

It includes:
-   `#version 450` and layout bindings as specified.
-   A guard for `gl_GlobalInvocationID.x >= N`.
-   Calculation of acceleration from all other bodies, skipping self-interaction.
-   Application of the `softening` parameter to avoid singularities.
-   Update of velocity using `acc * dt`.
-   Update of position using `vel * dt` (with the newly calculated velocity for leapfrog-like behavior).
-   Writing of both `position+mass` and `velocity` vec4s to the `outData` buffer.
