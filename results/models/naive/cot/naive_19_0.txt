Naive greedy voxel mining in C#: 1) Find the shallowest ore voxel (closest to surface). 2) Dig straight down from the surface to reach it, removing each voxel from top. 3) Dump waste rock far from the processing plant, spreading dumps to avoid spikes. 4) Deliver ore to the processing plant at (0,0). 5) Repeat until no more ore is reachable or op limit hit. Uses BFS pathfinding for each haul respecting slope constraint.
