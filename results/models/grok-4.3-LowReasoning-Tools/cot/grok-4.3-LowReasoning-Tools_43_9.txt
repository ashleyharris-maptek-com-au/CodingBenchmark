The task is to write an HLSL pixel shader for Vulkan that renders a UV checkerboard at 8x8 frequency on a unit sphere, applies Lambertian shading with factor (0.5 + 0.5*diffuse), and outputs alpha 1.0.

First, the checkerboard: alternating colors 0.9 and 0.2 based on parity of floor(uv * 8).

float2 p = floor(uv * 8.0);
