3.14159 is approx pi, so pi*16.

Stripes alternating red and blue based on that sin.

If sin >0 red, else blue? Alternating, 16 stripes per unit y I guess.

Then Lambertian: need to compute diffuse = max(0, dot(N,L))

N = normalize(normal)

L = normalize(lightPos.xyz - worldPos)

factor = 0.4 + 0.6*diffuse

color = red or blue * factor

alpha 1.0
