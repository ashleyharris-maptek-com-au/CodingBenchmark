The task is to implement toon/cel shading in SPIR-V assembly for a fragment shader on a unit sphere.

Key points:

- Quantize Lambertian diffuse term d = max(dot(N,L),0) into bands with thresholds 0.75/0.5/0.25 and values 1.0 0.7 0.4 0.2

- Multiply vertex color by the band value

- Output alpha 1.
