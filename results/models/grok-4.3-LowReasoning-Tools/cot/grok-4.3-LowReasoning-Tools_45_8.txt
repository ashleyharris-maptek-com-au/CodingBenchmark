The task is to write a SPIR-V fragment shader as raw binary data, encoded as a hex string. It must be a valid SPIR-V binary for a fragment shader implementing toon/cel shading.

The shader needs:

- Take inputs: worldPos (loc0), normal (loc1), uv (loc2 not used), tangent (loc3), color (loc4)

- UBO at set=0, binding=0 with various matrices and params, but for this, we mainly need lightPos probably.
