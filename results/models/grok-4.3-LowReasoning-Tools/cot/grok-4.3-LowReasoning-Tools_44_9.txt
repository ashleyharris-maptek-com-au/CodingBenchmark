The task is to write a GLSL fragment shader for Vulkan (GLSL 450) that renders a UV checkerboard on a unit sphere.
The task is to render a UV checkerboard at 8x8 frequency on a unit sphere, alternating colors 0.9 and 0.2 by parity, apply Lambertian shading with factor (0.5 + 0.5*diffuse), and output alpha 1.0.
