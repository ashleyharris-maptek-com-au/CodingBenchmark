The task is to write a SPIR-V assembly geometry shader for detecting and rendering silhouette/boundary edges as thick lines on a triangle mesh with adjacency.
The task requires writing complete SPIR-V assembly for a geometry shader that detects silhouette edges in a triangle mesh with adjacency and renders them as thick lines, plus the original triangle.

Input: InputTrianglesAdjacency, so gl_in has 6 vertices: indices 0,2,4 are main triangle vertices, 1 3 5 are adjacents.

For each edge of the triangle (0-2,2-4,4-0), check if adjacent vertex (1 for 0-2, 3 for 2-4, 5 for 4-0) is close to one of the main vertices to detect boundary.

If boundary, emit thick quad (triangle strip with 4 verts, thickness 0.015).

Then emit original triangle with dimmed colors.
