This shader implements the following features:

1. **Input handling**: Processes InputTrianglesAdjacency (6 vertices per invocation)
2. **Boundary detection**: Checks if adjacent vertices are degenerate (boundary edges)
3. **Original triangle rendering**: Emits the main triangle with dimmed colors (30% of original)
4. **Silhouette edge rendering**: For each boundary edge, emits a thick quad (4 vertices) as a triangle strip
5. **Edge styling**: White (1,1,1) colored thick lines with 0.015 thickness
6. **Output**: Triangle strip with up to 15 vertices (3 for triangle + 12 for up to 3 edges)

The shader uses helper functions to:
- Check position equality with epsilon tolerance
- Calculate perpendicular vectors for edge thickness
- Normalize and scale edge normals

The geometry shader can be assembled with `spirv-as` and used with a fixed vertex and fragment shader pipeline.
