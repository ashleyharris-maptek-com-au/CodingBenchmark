**Generating GLSL compute shader code**

I’m putting together a GLSL compute shader that calculates separation, alignment, and cohesion forces for boids using uniforms for radii, weights, bounds, and counts. The shader loops over neighbors, accumulates weighted forces based on distance thresholds, applies boundary forces smoothly near edges, clamps speeds, and writes updated positions and velocities to output buffers. I'll include detailed comments within the code for clarity and keep it fully self-contained.
