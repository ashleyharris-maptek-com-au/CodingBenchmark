There is a slight alignment issue in my manual assembly construction.
`%offset_p` uses `%thickness_vec2` (vec2), `%float_0`, `%float_0`.
`OpCompositeConstruct %v4float %v2float %float %float` is **INVALID**.
`OpCompositeConstruct` operands must be scalars (for vectors) or match the constituents.
To construct a vec4 from a vec2 and two floats, I must shuffle or extract/construct.
Correct way:
`%z = OpConstant %float 0`
`%w = OpConstant %float 0`
`%v2_offset = OpVectorTimesScalar ...`
`%o_x = OpCompositeExtract %float %v2_offset 0`
`%o_y = OpCompositeExtract %float %v2_offset 1`
`%offset_p = OpCompositeConstruct %v4float %o_x %o_y %z %w`

Or use `OpVectorShuffle`?
`%offset_v4 = OpVectorShuffle %v4float %v2_offset %v2_zero 0 1 2 3`?
