**Key implementation details:**

1. **Input primitive:** `InputTrianglesAdjacency` with array size 6
2. **Main triangle vertices:** indices 0, 2, 4 (the adjacency vertices are at 1, 3, 5)
3. **Three edges processed:** 0→2, 2→4, 4→0
4. **Each edge becomes a quad** (4 vertices) via triangle strip:
   - Two vertices at start point (±perpendicular offset)
   - Two vertices at end point (±perpendicular offset)
5. **Perpendicular calculation:** Cross product of edge direction with Z-axis for screen-space perpendicular
6. **Thickness:** 0.008 units in normalized device coordinates
7. **Color:** Average of the two endpoint vertex colors
8. **Total vertices:** 12 (3 edges × 4 vertices each)
9. **`OpEndPrimitive`** called after each edge's quad is complete
