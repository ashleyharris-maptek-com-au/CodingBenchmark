## Chess Bot Summary

**Features:**
1. **Complete FEN parsing** - Handles all FEN fields including piece placement, side to move, castling rights, en passant square, and move clocks

2. **Full move generation** - Supports all chess moves:
   - Pawn moves (single/double push, captures, en passant, promotion)
   - Knight, Bishop, Rook, Queen moves (with proper board wrapping detection)
   - King moves and castling (both kingside and queenside for both colors)

3. **Legal move validation** - Filters pseudo-legal moves to ensure king is never left in check

4. **Evaluation function** with:
   - Piece values (Pawn=100, Knight=320, Bishop=330, Rook=500, Queen=900)
   - Piece-square tables for positional bonuses

5. **Alpha-beta search** with:
   - Iterative deepening
   - Quiescence search (searches captures to avoid horizon effect)
   - MVV-LVA move ordering for better pruning

6. **Time management** - Runs in a separate thread with timeout, returns best move found

7. **Output formatting** - Correctly formats moves in the required disambiguated algebraic notation including:
   - Piece letter + source square + capture indicator + destination square
   - En passant suffix (`ep`)
   - Promotion (`/Q`, `/R`, `/B`, `/N`)
   - Castling (`O-O`, `O-O-O`)
   - Check (`+`) and checkmate (`#`) indicators
