This shader implements:
1. **InputLinesAdjacency** with 4 control points (P0-P3)
2. **Catmull-Rom-style interpolation** between P1 and P2 (simplified linear interpolation)
3. **4 sub-segments** (5 interpolated points)
4. **Perpendicular offset** of 0.02 for thickness
5. **10 vertices** emitted as a triangle strip (pos+perp, pos-perp for each point)
6. **Color interpolation** from color[1] to color[2]
7. Proper **gl_PerVertex** block handling for both input and output
8. All required **execution modes** and **entry point** declaration

The shader will create a smooth, thick curve by subdividing the line segment and generating a triangle strip that represents the curve with proper thickness.
