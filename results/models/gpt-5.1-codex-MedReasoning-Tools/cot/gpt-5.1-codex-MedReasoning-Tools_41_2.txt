**Generating SPIR-V assembly shader**

I’m creating a SPIR-V fragment shader that takes a vec2 input at location 2 and outputs a vec4 color with R and G from the input, B=0, A=1 at location 0. I’m assigning IDs methodically for types, variables, constants, and instructions, including decorations for the input and output locations, and setting the bound to include the highest ID. The final assembly follows SPIR-V conventions with OpCapability, OpMemoryModel, OpEntryPoint, and OpDecorate instructions.
