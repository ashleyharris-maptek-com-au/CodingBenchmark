**Drafting SPIR-V fragment shader assembly**

This plan lays out crafting a SPIR-V fragment shader that computes lighting with normalized normal, light, and view vectors from inputs and a uniform block. It covers types, constants, uniforms, vector math using GLSL standard extended instructions, and careful handling of the UBO struct layout per std140, culminating in composing the final output color. I’m working through instructions and decorations methodically to produce the full assembly code.
