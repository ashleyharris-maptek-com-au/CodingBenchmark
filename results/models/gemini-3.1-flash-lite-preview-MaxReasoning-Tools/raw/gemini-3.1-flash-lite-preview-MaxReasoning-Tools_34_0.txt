To solve the Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem (CVRP), I implemented a greedy heuristic:
1. Parse input including customers, depot coordinates, and vehicle capacity.
2. Iteratively build routes by adding the nearest unvisited customer that fits the current vehicle load.
3. Once a route is full or no more customers can fit, move to the next route.
4. Finally, calculate the total tour distance and output the routes.

This heuristic efficiently balances capacity constraints with distance minimization.
