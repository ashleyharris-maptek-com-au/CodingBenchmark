The solution uses:
1.  **Dreyfus-Wagner DP:** `dp[mask][i]` to store the minimum weight to connect a subset of terminals represented by `mask`, with vertex `i` included in the tree.
2.  **Dijkstra:** For efficient relaxation within the DP transitions.
3.  **Backtracking:** A recursive function `get_edges` to reconstruct the edges forming the optimal Steiner tree from the DP table.
