**Developing a packing algorithm**

I'm focused on creating a scalable solver for rectangle packing. I'll use the Guillotine packing method with either shelf or skyline approaches and implement the MaxRects heuristic, particularly the Best Short Side Fit, including rotations. For handling up to 50,000 rectangles efficiently in O(n*m) time, I'll sort rectangles by their max side and try to fit them in existing boards. If they don't fit, I'll create a new board and apply the MaxRects algorithm, ensuring to keep everything optimized and organized.
