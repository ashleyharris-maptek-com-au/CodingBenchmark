### 🔑 Key Features:

**Algorithm: Force-Directed Layout with Spatial Hashing**
- **Spatial Hashing**: Reduces neighbor queries from O(N²) to O(N log N)
- **Spring Forces**: Hooke's law for edges
- **Coulomb Repulsion**: All-pair repulsion
- **Damping**: 0.85 factor for convergence
- **Boundary Constraints**: [-500, 500] coordinate range

**Adaptive Algorithm Selection:**
- **Small graphs (≤ 100)**: 3000 iterations, high accuracy
- **Medium graphs (101-10000)**: 1500 iterations, balanced
- **Large graphs (> 10000)**: 600 iterations, optimized

**Edge Cases Handled:**
- ✅ Self-loops (skipped)
- ✅ Out-of-bounds nodes (skipped)
- ✅ Parallel edges (sum forces)
- ✅ Disconnected components
- ✅ Empty graphs
