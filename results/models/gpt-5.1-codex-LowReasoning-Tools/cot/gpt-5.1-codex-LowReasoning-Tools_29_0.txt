**Designing maximum clique heuristics in C#**

I’m preparing to create a C# solution for finding maximum cliques in huge graphs—up to a million vertices. Since exact algorithms are impractical this large, I’ll combine heuristics like degeneracy ordering, bitset operations for smaller subproblems, branch-and-bound with pivoting and coloring, and fallback heuristics under time constraints. I’m also considering leveraging multi-threading with tasks to run repeated local searches concurrently for better results. The goal is a single, integrated C# implementation combining these strategies.
