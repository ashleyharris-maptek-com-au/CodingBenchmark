**Drafting SPIR-V fragment shader assembly**

I'm working out the detailed SPIR-V assembly needed for a fragment shader that shades using world position, normals, and a light position from a uniform buffer. This involves defining all necessary types, constants, uniform blocks, inputs, and calculations like normalization, dot product, mixing colors, and outputting the final color. I’m carefully building the structure with correct decorations, offsets, vector operations, and GLSL450 extended instructions, aiming for a full explicit SPIR-V text assembly.
