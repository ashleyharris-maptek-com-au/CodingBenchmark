**Designing rectangle packing with MaxRects heuristic**

I'm sketching a solution that uses a hybrid approach: for up to 200 rectangles, a Best-Fit Decreasing method with guillotine splits, and beyond that, a MaxRects heuristic based on the best short side fit. I plan to represent boards with free rectangles, handle rotations, and iteratively place rectangles into existing boards or create new ones. The approach involves sorting rectangles, selecting placements minimizing leftover space, splitting free spaces accordingly, pruning redundant free rectangles, and efficiently managing large inputs up to 50k rectangles. I'll build data structures and algorithms carefully to keep it both correct and scalable.
