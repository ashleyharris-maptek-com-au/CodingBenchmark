**Designing exact minimum bisection algorithm**

I’m thinking through implementing an exact minimum bisection solution using bitmask DP and combinational enumeration. It’s tricky since the problem is NP-hard, but for around 20 nodes, iterating subsets of size n/2 with efficient bit operations and Gosper’s hack for next combinations should work. I plan to represent adjacency with u128 bitmasks and carefully count crossing edges to find minimum cuts. This approach balances clarity and efficiency given the theoretical constraints, and I’ll handle reading input, edge cases, and outputting results accordingly. Let’s see how this comes together!
