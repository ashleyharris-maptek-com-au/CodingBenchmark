**Devising heuristic fire-cutting strategy**

I'm working out a heuristic approach to block fire near towns by cutting forest cells adjacent to them, trying to maximize protection given a cut budget. The plan involves computing shortest paths from ignitions to towns with BFS and then prioritizing cutting cells next to the highest-value towns, aiming for simplicity over exact minimal cuts. I'll translate this into TypeScript with clear structure and efficient BFS computations to meet the user's needs.
