**Drafting SPIR-V fragment shader**

I'm preparing a SPIR-V fragment shader that takes a per-vertex color input at location 4 (a vec3), adds an alpha of 1.0, and outputs a vec4 color at location 0. I’m planning to declare needed types, variables, constants, and operations step-by-step, including OpLoad of input, extracting vector components, constructing the vec4 output, and setting proper declarations and decorations. This plan lays groundwork for a clear direct SPIR-V assembly pass without uniforms.
