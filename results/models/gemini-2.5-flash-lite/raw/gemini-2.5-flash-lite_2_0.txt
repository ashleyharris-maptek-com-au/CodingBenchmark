The Chinese Postman Problem solver has been implemented in C#.

The solution includes:
1.  **Graph Representation**: Adjacency lists for efficient graph traversal.
2.  **Odd-Degree Node Identification**: Pre-processing to determine if augmentation is needed.
3.  **Graph Augmentation**: A greedy Minimum Weight Perfect Matching (MWPM) strategy using Dijkstra's algorithm for shortest paths between odd-degree nodes to make the graph Eulerian.
4.  **Eulerian Circuit Construction**: Hierholzer's algorithm to generate the required route.
