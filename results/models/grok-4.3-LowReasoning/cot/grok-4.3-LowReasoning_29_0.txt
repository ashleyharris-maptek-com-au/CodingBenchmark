The task is to write a C# program that solves the Maximum Clique problem for graphs from 20 vertices up to 1 million, handling sparse to dense graphs, all within 5 minutes.
The problem requires finding the maximum clique in an undirected graph, and it must be exact: the largest one, no larger exists. But for large graphs up to 1M vertices, exact is impossible in time.

It says "the same algorithm must work efficiently across ALL graph complexities, from 20 vertices to 1 million.
