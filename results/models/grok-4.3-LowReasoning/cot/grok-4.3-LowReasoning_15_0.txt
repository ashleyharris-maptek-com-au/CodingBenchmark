The task is to solve a "Shadow Covering problem with tetrahedrons in Rust." It involves covering a polygon on the z=0 plane with shadows cast by tetrahedrons from a sun direction.
The problem is to cover a polygon on the ground with shadows cast by tetrahedrons under a sun direction. The tetras are placed in 3D, oriented somehow, and their shadows on the z=0 plane should cover the polygon without overlapping each other, and minimize the number, with scale <=10.
