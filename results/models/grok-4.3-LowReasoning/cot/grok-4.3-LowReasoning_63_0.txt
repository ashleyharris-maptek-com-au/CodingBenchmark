I need to output assignments of jobs to robots...The problem is to assign warehouse pick jobs to robots on a grid. The grid has open floors (.) and blocked shelving (#). Robots have positions (x,y), jobs have (x,y) and priority.

I need to assign each job to one robot, and for each robot, the jobs assigned to it will be in some order, probably sorted by priority or something? But the output is lists of jobIds for each robot.

The grader uses shortest grid walking distance through open cells, and compares total travel to a deterministic greedy reference.

So, I need to produce an assignment where the total sum of distances (shortest paths) is comparable or good compared to greedy.
