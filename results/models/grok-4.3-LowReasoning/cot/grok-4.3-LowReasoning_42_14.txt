The task is to write a SPIR-V assembly geometry shader for Vulkan 1.0 using spirv-as.
First, the entry point: OpEntryPoint Geometry %main "main" listing ALL interface variables.

Interface variables: gl_in, gl_out, and the user-defined inputs like worldPos[], normal[], color[].

User-defined: layout(location=0) in vec3 worldPos[]; etc., so they are inputs.

 layout(location=0) out vec3 outColor;

So variables: %gl_in, %gl_out, %worldPos_in, %normal_in, %color_in, %outColor.
