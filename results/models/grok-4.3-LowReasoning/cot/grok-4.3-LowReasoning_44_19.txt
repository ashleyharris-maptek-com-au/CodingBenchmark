The task is to write a GLSL fragment shader for Vulkan using GLSL 450. It needs to implement Phong lighting with specific parameters.
The task is to complete Phong lighting with colored materials: ambient (0.1, 0.05, 0.05), diffuse uses vertex color with max(dot(N,L),0), specular uses (1,1,1) with shininess 32 and R=reflect(-L,N).
