Let's search my knowledge for typical SPIR-V geometry shader for thick lines.

Typically, to render thick lines in geometry shader:

- Input line from A to B

- Compute dir = B - A

- Normalize dir

- Perp = normalize(cross(dir, normal)) where normal is view direction or (0,0,1 1) to get 2D perp.

- Then offset = perp * thickness

- Then emit A + offset, A - offset, B + offset, B - offset

But since in clip space, offset in clip space is not constant thickness.
