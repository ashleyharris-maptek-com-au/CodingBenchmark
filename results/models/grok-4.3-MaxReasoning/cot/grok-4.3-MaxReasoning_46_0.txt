But it says "leapfrog/Euler", leapfrog is symplectic integrator.

In standard leapfrog for N-body:

- Compute acc from positions

- Update vel 1/2 step: vel += 0.5 * acc * dt

- Update pos += vel * dt

- Update vel += 0.5 * acc * dt

But here, since it's one shader, and "advanced by acc*dt" for vel, "by vel*dt" for pos -- probably simple Euler: pos += vel*dt; vel += acc*dt
