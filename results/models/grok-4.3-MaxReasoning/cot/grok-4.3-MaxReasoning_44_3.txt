The task is: "Pass through the per-vertex color directly with alpha 1.0."
#version 450
layout(location = 0) out vec4 outColor;
layout(location = 4) in vec3 color;
void main() {
    outColor = vec4(color, 1.0);
}


## Explanation

The fragment shader begins with #version 450 to ensure compatibility with Vulkan  GLSL 4.50 semantics. The required output declaration layout(location = 0) out vec4 outColor; is placed first to bind the RGBA result to the framebuffer location 0.
