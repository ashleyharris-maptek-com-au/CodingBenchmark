The task is to write a GLSL fragment shader for Vulkan. It renders on a unit sphere with inputs from the vertex shader: worldPos, normal, uv, tangent, color, and a UBO with matrices, lightPos, cameraP...The task is to render a 6x6 UV polka-dot pattern on a unit sphere. The sphere is unit, so worldPos is on the sphere, |worldPos| = 1.

Inputs: worldPos, normal (may need normalize), uv, tangent, color.

UBO has model, view projection, lightPos (5,5,5,1), cameraPos (0,0,3,1), params (0,0.5,0,0)

Output outColor rgba8unorm, so vec4 with alpha 1.0

For polka-dot: 6x6 UV grid, cell space fract(uv*6), dist to (0.5,0.5) <0.3 yellow dot 1,0.8,0 else bg 0.1,0.1,0.3

Then Lambertian: N=normalize(normal), L=normalize(lightPos.
