- The weight (`float_1_9` or `float_1_36`)
- The `cu` calculation (`cx*ux + cy*uy`)
- The neighbor offset (`x + cx`, `y + cy`) with proper modulo

The modulo is handled using `OpSMod` (as requested) for periodic boundary conditions. The shader correctly implements one full LBM step (BGK collision + streaming) with the D2Q9 model.
