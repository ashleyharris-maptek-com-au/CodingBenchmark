I have developed a functional greedy solver in `solver.cpp` that addresses the 3D packing problem for regular tetrahedrons. The approach utilizes a grid-based search within the container's bounding box to identify potential placement locations and a bounding-sphere check to ensure no overlaps occur between the packed tetrahedrons, adhering to the performance and functionality requirements.

The code is compliant with C++20 standards and is designed to handle the specified input/output formats.
