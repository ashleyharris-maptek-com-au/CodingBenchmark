The Rust K-Means clustering solver has been successfully implemented and compiled. -   **Data Structures:** `Point` and `Centroid` structs with necessary helper functions for distance calculation and centroid updates.
-   **Centroid Initialization:** Employs reservoir sampling for robust initial centroid selection, especially with large datasets.
-   **K-Means Iteration:** Iteratively refines centroids until convergence or a maximum number of iterations is reached.
-   **Final Assignment:** A final pass over the data (from memory or the temporary file) to assign each point to its closest converged centroid and output the result.
