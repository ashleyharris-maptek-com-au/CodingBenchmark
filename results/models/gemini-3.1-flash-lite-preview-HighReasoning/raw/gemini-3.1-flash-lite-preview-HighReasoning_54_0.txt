### Plan Summary: Autopilot for ILS Approach and Autoland
The design leverages a modular architecture:
- **`ILSController`**: Utilizes PID control loops to track lateral (localizer) and vertical (glideslope) deviations.
- **`AutolandManager`**: Coordinates the landing phase, managing the flare and throttle.
- **`Autopilot`**: Acts as the central controller for inputs and outputs.
- **Verification**: Will focus on validating glideslope capture and the flare maneuver using a simulated flight loop.
