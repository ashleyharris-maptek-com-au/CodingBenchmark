The task is to implement hemisphere ambient lighting with specific parameters. Let me break down what's needed:

1. Hemisphere ambient lighting:
   - skyColor = (0.4, 0.6, 1.0)
   - groundColor = (0.3, 0.15, 0.05)
   - Blend factor = N.y * 0.5 + 0.5 (where N is the normalized world-space normal)
   - This means when N.y = 1 (pointing up), blend = 1.0 → full sky color
   - When N.y = -1 (pointing down), blend = 0.0 → full ground color

2. Small diffuse term: 0.3 * d (where d is the diffuse lighting from the light)

3. Output alpha = 1.0

Let me write the shader:

- Normalize the normal
- Compute hemisphere blend factor
- Compute ambient = mix(groundColor, skyColor, N.y * 0.5 + 0.5)
- Compute diffuse lighting: d = max(dot(N, L), 0.0) where L is the light direction
- Add 0.3 * d to the ambient
- Output with alpha 1.0

The light position is ubo.lightPos.xyz = (5.0, 5.0, 5.0), and we have worldPos, so:
L = normalize(ubo.lightPos.xyz - worldPos)

Final color = ambient + 0.3 * d
