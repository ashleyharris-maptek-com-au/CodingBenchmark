The user wants a GLSL fragment shader implementing Blinn-Phong shading. Let me write this carefully.

Key elements:
- N = normalized normal (from vertex shader, needs normalize)
- L = normalized light direction (from worldPos to lightPos)
- V = normalized view direction (from worldPos to cameraPos)
- H = normalize(L + V)
- diffuse = max(dot(N, L), 0)
- specular = pow(max(dot(N, H), 0), 64)
- intensity = 0.1 + 0.6*diffuse + 0.8*specular
- output: vec4(intensity, intensity, intensity, 1.0)
