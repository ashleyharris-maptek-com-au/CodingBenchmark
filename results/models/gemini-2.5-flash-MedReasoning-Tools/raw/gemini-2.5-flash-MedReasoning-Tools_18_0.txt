2.  **Stoer-Wagner Algorithm (for Min-Cut > 1):**
    *   If no bridge is found, the minimum cut is greater than 1. For such cases, I implemented the Stoer-Wagner algorithm. This algorithm provides an exact global minimum cut for undirected graphs.
