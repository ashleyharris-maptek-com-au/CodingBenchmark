Using k-nearest neighbor z-score analysis in Rust: 1) Parse all drillhole samples with their 3D positions. 2) For each sample, find k nearest neighbors from other holes. 3) Calculate mean and stddev of each property from neighbors. 4) Compute z-score: |value - mean| / stddev. 5) Flag entries with z-score > 3.0 as suspects (3-sigma outliers). 6) Sort by confidence (z-score) descending.
