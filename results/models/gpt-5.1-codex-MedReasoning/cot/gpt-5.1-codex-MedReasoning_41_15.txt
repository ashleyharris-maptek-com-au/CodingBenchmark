**Designing GLSL shading algorithm**

I'm planning a GLSL fragment shader that computes lighting by normalizing input normals and calculating the dot product with a light direction derived from uniform buffer light position minus world position. I'll use this dot product to mix cool and warm colors smoothly, carefully defining vector and float constants, handling types, loading inputs and uniforms, and applying the GLSL extended instruction set for normalization and dot product. This involves detailed SPIR-V-style instruction planning for variables, decorations, and arithmetic to build the final colored output.
