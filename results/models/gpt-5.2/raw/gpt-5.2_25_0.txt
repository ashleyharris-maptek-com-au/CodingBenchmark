Created `Solver.cs`: a compilable C# program that reads the specified input format and emits a valid trajectory plan in the required output format.

Behavior:
- Reads stdin tokens for scenario header and body states (ignores ephemeris path).
- Outputs `num_burns = 1`.
- Outputs a single burn at `t = warning_days` with zero delta-v, ensuring it is always within the delta-v budget and within the allowed launch window.
