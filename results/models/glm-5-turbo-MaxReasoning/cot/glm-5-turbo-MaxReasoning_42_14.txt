Now the main function:

1. Load worldPos[0], worldPos[2], worldPos[4]
2. Compute edge vectors: e1 = p2 - p0, e2 = p4 - p0
3. Compute cross product manually
4. Normalize to get face normal N
5. Compute light direction L = normalize(vec3(1,1,1))
6. Compute diffuse = max(dot(N, L), 0.0)
7. Load colors[0], colors[2], colors[4] and average them
8. Compute final color = base * (0.2 + 0.8 * diffuse)
9. For each vertex i in [0, 2, 4]:
   - Load gl_in[i].gl_Position
   - Store to gl_out.gl_Position
   - Store final color to outColor
   - EmitVertex
